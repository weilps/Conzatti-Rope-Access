Services

At Conzatti Rope Access, we are proud to offer a comprehensive range of high-quality rope access services to meet your every need.

Our Signature Services

Facade remedial

Facade Remedial is our expert service, delivering outstanding results for all your residential, commercial and industrial painting needs, including industrial coating and texture coating

Concrete works

Fix or transform your concrete structures with our expert Concrete Works services. We will perform all works from any repairs (concrete, mortar, crack, render) to whole Facade render.

Technical Rigging

Our expert team at Conzatti Rope Access brings over 10 years of experience in both urban and geotechnical rigging to your job site. We guarantee safe and technical rigging solutions for all your needs, no matter if you need to hoist something on the roof or access hard-to-reach areas.

Standby Rescus

We ensure the safety of our clients and their job sites by providing trained and equipped personnel ready to respond to any emergency situation.

Work at Heights consultant & audit

Our experienced team can provide comprehensive Work at Heights consulting and auditing services, ensuring that your job is compliant with all relevant safety regulations and guidelines. We work closely with our clients to identify potential hazards and provide recommendations for improvement, to create a safe and secure environment for everyone.

Remote work

Our team is equipped to tackle even the most challenging of projects, no matter where they may be located. Whether you need work done in a Victorian remote area or interstate, we have the expertise and resources necessary to get the job done right.
